Welcome to Let's Study

This is your hub for connecting with fellow students to enhance your academic journey. Discover two key features: a sign-up board for in-person study groups and online forums for virtual study sessions. Here all will benefit from collaborative learning environments that cater to your schedule and learning preferences, fostering academic success through shared knowledge and community support.

In-Person Meetings

Here you can find meeting times set up by fellow students for your specific classes - Don't see any meetings? Set up your own meeting time and place that others can join!

Online Groups

Here you can interact with others in your course with a online forum. You can also find other students online contact information to start studying over microsoft teams or zoom!
